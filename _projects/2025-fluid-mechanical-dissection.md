**Overview**

In a group of 5, I took apart a weed wacker and analyzed its fluid mechanical systems. We then created a video summarizing our analysis. The video is shown below:

**Individual Contribution**

I worked on disassembling the carburetor, photographing the parts of the weed wacker, identifying and researching components, and writing the script. While taking apart the carburetor, I identified the venturi, inlet, and outlet. While my team members took apart other parts of the weed wacker, I took pictures of the components and took notes on our discussion to reference while making the visuals and the script for the video. I drafted a diagram of the path that the air and fuel take through each component of the weed wacker using photos, which was later converted by one of my teammates into the block diagram seen in the video for ease of visibility. I identified the flywheel and diaphragm pump and researched flywheels to ensure that our analysis aligned with their usual purposes. I also wrote the following parts of the script: introduction, parts overview, 2 stroke engine, flywheel, and the carburetor purpose and overview. I also voiced the first section of the video up until the end of the 2 stroke engine section.
